# FUTURE_DS_02
# 📊 Customer Retention & Churn Analysis
## 🔍 Project Overview
This project analyzes customer churn and retention patterns for a subscription-based business. The goal is to identify why customers leave, understand retention trends, and provide actionable insights to improve customer loyalty.

---


## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze customer churn patterns
- Identify key factors affecting customer retention
- Study customer lifetime (tenure)
- Provide business recommendations to reduce churn

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, Charts, Dashboard)

---

## 📁 Dataset
The dataset includes customer information such as:
- CustomerID
- Gender
- Tenure
- Contract Type
- Payment Method
- Monthly Charges
- Churn (Yes/No)

---

## 📊 Analysis & Visualizations
The following charts were created:

1. **Customer Tenure Trend (Line Chart)**  
   Shows how customer retention changes over time.

2. **Churn Distribution (Pie Chart)**  
   Displays the percentage of customers who churned vs retained.

3. **Churn by Customer Segment (Bar Chart)**  
   Compares churn across different categories like contract or payment method.

4. **Customer Lifetime Distribution (Bar Chart)**  
   Shows how long customers stay with the company.

---

## 🔑 Key Insights
- Customers with longer tenure are less likely to churn  
- Month-to-month contracts have higher churn rates  
- Certain payment methods show higher churn behavior  
- Majority of customers are retained  

---

## 💡 Recommendations
- Encourage long-term contracts to reduce churn  
- Improve services for high-churn segments  
- Provide offers or discounts for at-risk customers  
- Enhance customer engagement strategies  

---

## 🚀 Conclusion
This analysis helps businesses understand customer behavior and take data-driven actions to improve retention and reduce churn.
